Diagnostic Engine

A bounded knee symptom reasoning system.

The engine converts a knee complaint into controlled evidence, asks one focused follow-up question at a time, and stops with a shortlist, fallback, or safety escalation.

I built it to test how far a narrow medical reasoning loop can go when the important parts are registry-defined instead of hidden inside one large prompt.

01 53 symptom signals and 28 follow-up prompts.

02 Registry-defined symptoms, question mappings, disease definitions, contradiction logic, and safety exits.

03 Persistent sessions and append-only reasoning ledger.

04 Proof: live demo / GitHub repo.
