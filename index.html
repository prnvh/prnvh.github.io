Operating Posture

How I work.

01 · Governance

Governance before cleverness

Raw model output is never the final authority. Important state has to be promoted, validated, and made structurally legible.

02 · Failure

Failure should arrive with a name

I prefer systems that reject, defer, or surface uncertainty clearly over systems that smooth over it and silently drift.

03 · Writing

Writeups are part of the work

If a system deserves trust, it should survive being explained in public, with architecture, tradeoffs, and failure boundaries made explicit.
